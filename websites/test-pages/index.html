Meet the Species

The World of Tropical Fish

Tropical fish are aquatic animals found in tropical environments worldwide, commonly kept in heated aquariums for their vibrant coloration and unique behaviors.

Warm Habitats

Tropical fish thrive in warm water environments, typically ranging from 22°C to 30°C (72°F to 86°F).

Vibrant Colors

Their stunning appearance comes from specialized pigmented and iridescent cells called chromatophores.
